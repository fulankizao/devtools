About BLAKE2b

BLAKE2b is a cryptographic hash function designed by Jean-Philippe Aumasson and Samuel Neves. It is optimized for 64-bit platforms and is faster than SHA-2 while providing equivalent security. BLAKE2b is used in the Zcash cryptocurrency and other high-performance applications.
