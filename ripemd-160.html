About RIPEMD-160

RIPEMD-160 is a 160-bit cryptographic hash function designed by Hans Dobbertin and a team of researchers. It is primarily known for its use in Bitcoin address generation (SHA256 → RIPEMD-160). RIPEMD-160 provides a balance of security and shorter output length.
