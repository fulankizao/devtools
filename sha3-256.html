About SHA3-256

SHA3-256 is a cryptographic hash function standardized by NIST in FIPS 202 (2015). It is based on the Keccak sponge construction and produces a 256-bit (64 hex char) hash. SHA3-256 is designed to be resistant to length extension attacks.
