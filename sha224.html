About SHA224

SHA-224 is a truncated version of SHA-256, producing a 224-bit (56 hex char) hash. It is standardized in FIPS PUB 180-4 and is suitable for environments where a shorter hash length is required while maintaining backward compatibility with SHA-2 codebases.
