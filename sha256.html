About SHA256

SHA256 (Secure Hash Algorithm 256-bit) is a cryptographic hash function that produces a 64-character hexadecimal string. It is widely used for data integrity verification, digital signatures, and password hashing. SHA256 is part of the SHA-2 family designed by the NSA.
